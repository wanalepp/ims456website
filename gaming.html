Gaming

Video games has been my happy place ever since I was 3 or 4 years old. These are a few games that I would consider to be my favourites:

osu!

osu! is a free-to-play rhythm game developed by ppy Pty Ltd. It features four different game modes that require players to click or tap on circles, sliders, and spinners in time with the beat of a song. The game has a large community of users who create their own custom beatmaps for players to enjoy, and a ranking system that allows players to compete and earn points. osu! is popular in the anime and gaming communities and can be played using a mouse, tablet, keyboard, or touchscreen.

Kingdom Hearts

Kingdom Hearts is a popular action role-playing game series developed and published by Square Enix. It follows the story of Sora, a young boy who is searching for his friends and trying to protect the world from darkness. Along the way, he teams up with characters from the Final Fantasy series and Disney franchises and travels to various worlds based on Disney movies. The series is known for its blend of action-packed gameplay and heartwarming storytelling, and has spawned numerous sequels and spin-off games.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. In the game, up to 60 players are dropped onto a map and must fight to be the last team standing. Players can choose to play as one of a roster of unique characters, each with their own abilities and playstyles, and must work together to gather resources, defend their team, and ultimately be the last ones standing. The game features a variety of weapons and equipment to choose from and has gained a large player base due to its fast-paced gameplay and character-driven narrative.
