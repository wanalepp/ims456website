Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Jalan Tiga (2012-2016)

I studied in Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Jalan Tiga Bandar Baru Bangi from 2012 until 2016. When I was 16 (Form 4) I was in the accounting and arabic class which I took 11 Subjects for my Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia. Out of the 11, I scored 5A's in total.

I had fond memories in this school as it builds my character and my passion for accounting. I had teachers who helped me realize what my passion was and guided me with the path to become a successful accountant.
